Meet the Candidates

Donald J Trump

Donald J. Trump, the 45th U.S. President (2017-2021), is a businessman and media personality known for his unorthodox leadership style. A Republican, he prioritized tax reforms, deregulation, and immigration policies. Before politics, Trump built a real estate empire and hosted The Apprentice. His tenure remains highly polarizing in U.S. history.

Kamala Harris

Kamala Harris is the 49th Vice President of the United States and the first woman, Black, and South Asian American to hold the office. A former California Attorney General and U.S. Senator, she champions civil rights, healthcare reform, and climate action, advocating for equality and justice across the nation.
